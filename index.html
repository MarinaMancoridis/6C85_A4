Proposition: Between 1995 and 2019, the economic value of China’s rail system expanded at a rate that significantly surpassed that of the United States.

FOR the Proposition

Design Decisions and Rationale:

  • Rationale 1 (Data Transformation): I made the decision to plot the running sum of the average value of the railroads. This intentionally misleads the viewer into thinking that the growth in China's rail system value is exponential. The cumulative sum naturally produces an exponential-like curve, subtly misleading the viewer into perceiving a steeper increase than what truly exists. I score this decision as -1.5 because the data transformation is inherently misleading in a way that requires careful analysis to recognize.
  • Rationale 2 (Textual Content): Including factual content in text boxes enhances the perception that the visualization is well-researched. However, the facts were selectively chosen to strengthen the argument—highlighting more positive aspects and higher-frequency data about China's rail system compared to the United States. I score this decision as -0.5 because, while the information is factual and adds valuable historical context to the illustration, it is selectively presented in a way that introduces bias.
  • Rationale 3 (Icons): The image features only two icons: a high-speed train emoji on the China line and a toy train emoji on the United States line. This visual choice subtly suggests that the U.S. rail system is outdated or inferior compared to China’s, influencing the viewer’s perception of their relative economic progress. I rate this decision -0.5 because it subtly guides the reader toward a conclusion about the two rail systems, though its impact remains relatively minor.

  • Rationale 5: choice of the standard line plot was intentional! seems like it's very objective since it's familiar!
